The Challenge

Inspiring the next generation of bioscientists through games

Almost every other field in STEM is well-represented in modern videogames - all except for biotech.

As a team formed of highly qualified synthetic biologists, we've set out to change that.

U-GENE

COMBAT RPG

A rogue-like based around self-augmentation.
Loved Risk of Rain? Bioshock? Or have a favourite DNA assembly standard? U-Gene is for U.

Stranded on an alien planet, U-Gene must expose himself to monster DNA to power-up and survive. But how much humanity will he leave behind?

NARRATIVE RPG
